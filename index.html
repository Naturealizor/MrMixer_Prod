Why repairing and servicing your mixer is better than buying new!

Re-greasing is the service we started this business with. So many of our customers had nowhere to go with a failing or even broken mixer. A lot of the time, all they need is to be opened up and given a little lovin’ and they’ll be working as good as new. With as many moving parts on the inside, making sure everything is well lubricated and still in good shape is essential to making sure these wonderful machines last for years and years.

If it’s been a while since your machine has been serviced, or if it never has been, there’s a good chance the oil inside has started to separate and harden. If this gets bad enough, the gears will start to grind and even start to chip and break apart sending little shards of metal flying everywhere. This could easily put a mixer out of commission, which is why re-greasing is so important. For how much these machines cost, having them properly maintained every couple of years will greatly increase their life, and keep you from having to shell out another $300+ on a new one!!
